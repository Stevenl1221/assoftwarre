AS-IMAGE™ Ultrasound Image Management & Archival Solution (PACS)

AS-IMAGE™ delivers a state-of-the-art ultrasound image management and archival solution that is scalable, robust, and efficiently manages ultrasound images. AS-IMAGE is designed to work cooperatively with your AS Software reporting solution.

AS-IMAGE is vendor neutral and compatible with all ultrasound equipment manufacturers. AS-IMAGE will streamline your image management and ultrasound reporting workflows to maximize productivity and enhance patient care.

Integrated Reporting and Image Management

Leverage the power of your AS Reporting tool and AS-IMAGE to build professional diagnostic reports in seconds. Conveniently review images in high resolution DICOM format that are linked directly to your ultrasound reports. AS-IMAGE allows you to compare today's images with previous studies as well as previous pregnancies. Embed your selected images and finalize your reports with a click of a mouse.

Stand-Alone PACS or Integrate with Existing PACS

AS-IMAGE is a configurable solution to efficiently manage ultrasound images and retention requirements. Our technology enables you to use AS-IMAGE as a stand-alone PACS solution or fully integrate with your hospital or enterprise PACS.

AS-IMAGE provides the tools to maximize the efficiency of your image storage and archival needs, while automating the management of your short-term and long-term image storage through local storage, optical disc, NAS, SAN, hospital or enterprise PACS.

Powerful and Comprehensive Image Viewer

AS-IMAGE offers the tools to efficiently manage your ultrasound images and maximize productivity. AS-IMAGE will enable you to: manage high resolution images directly from ultrasound modalities, use work lists customized for sonographers, physicians and administrators; review still and full motion images; utilize 3D/4D/5D reconstruction packages; use system controls to zoom in/out of images and adjust brightness, contrast and playback speed; measure and annotate images; remove patient information from images for anonymity; fix rejected studies; edit study information; and process offline images.

Academic and Research Support Tools

AS-IMAGE provides the tools to manage ultrasound image studies in support of your practice management, accreditation reviews, academic presentations and research. These tools will enable you to quickly search on your customized image study labels (e.g., to find a particular pathology); export selected images or the entire studies to share with your colleagues; and prepare case studies.

Scalable Systems for Today's Healthcare Demands

AS-IMAGE is developed to be a scalable and robust image solution. We provide image management to a wide variety of organizations: single office practices, multi-site practices, and complex healthcare enterprises with units located in multiple sites. Our solution is designed to integrate with your healthcare information systems, interface with your ultrasound modalities and scale with your organizational needs. AS Software collaborates with its clients and continues to invest in the development of solutions to meet the changing needs of today's healthcare professionals.

Seamless Ultrasound Modality Integration

AS-IMAGE is vendor neutral and compatible with all ultrasound equipment manufacturers. It is fully DICOM compliant and supports single-frame, cine loops, and 3D/4D/5D volumes. Use DICOM modality work lists to upload scheduled patient data to your ultrasound machine. Automatically download ultrasound measurements via DICOM-SR or offline serial/parallel/USB communication. AS-IMAGE provides a fully integrated solution that streamlines data entry to reduce errors.

Healthcare Information Technology Regulation Compliance

AS Software's products comply with the applicable healthcare information technology (HIT) regulations: the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) privacy and security requirements; and electronic signature technology in accordance with 21 CFR part 11 FDA regulations for electronic records and electronic signatures. In addition, our system is registered with the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA) pursuant to Title 21, 807 et seq. of the United States Code of Federal Regulations and is compliant with FDA 510(k) requirements.
