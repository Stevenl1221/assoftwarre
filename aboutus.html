About AS Software, Inc.

AS Software develops and supports structured reporting, image management, and EMR solutions for three major market segments: OB/GYN & MFM, Radiology and Vascular. Its suite of products offers customers flexibility and scalability, while providing needed access to advanced functionality modules such as DICOM image archiving (PACS) and electronic medical record (EMR) solutions.

AS Software delivers state-of-the-art ultrasound reporting and image management designed for OB/GYN, MFM, Radiology and Vascular. It is designed to be intuitive, easy to use, scalable and robust, utilizing a structured, clinical database to efficiently manage ultrasound information.

The AS-PERINATAL™ EMR integrates seamlessly with the AS-OBGYN™ ultrasound reporting and image management to provide OB/GYN and MFM practices at both hospitals and private clinics with a complete EMR and ultrasound reporting solution.

AS Software seamlessly Interfaces with existing healthcare information systems and successfully meets the reporting and image storage needs of any size practice. It is vendor neutral and compatible with all ultrasound equipment manufacturers.

AS Software was founded in 1991 and has consistently demonstrated that it has the technology, experience, and commitment to customer satisfaction to become one of the leading providers of ultrasound structured reporting solutions. AS Software is based in Englewood Cliffs, New Jersey, and is a rapidly growing and market-leading provider of integrated, cost-effective, healthcare information technology (HIT) solutions for ultrasound structured reporting and image management. AS Software continues its investment to develop and extend its product suites to support additional sub-specialties (e.g., cardiology) and electronic medical record (EMR) solutions.

Mission Statement

To become the premier provider of ultrasound reporting and image management solutions for OB/GYN & MFM, Radiology, Vascular, and other identified ultrasound sub-specialty market segments.

The AS Software team is and shall remain passionate about its goal of consistently delivering value-added features and functionality to its clients without compromising quality, while in turn helping clients maximize practice productivity, improve communication with referring physicians, and overall enhance patient care.

Our mission includes the delivery of intuitive, easy to use ultrasound image management and reporting solutions and superior customer support, both exceeding client expectations. Superior customer support is not only AS Software's commitment, it is the cornerstone of our relationship with clients. To learn more, please visit our Client Testimonials.

Healthcare Information Technology, HIPAA & HITECH Act

AS Software products comply with healthcare information technology (HIT) regulations including the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) privacy and security requirements and electronic signature technology in accordance with 21 CFR part 11 FDA regulations for electronic records and electronic signatures. Our system is also registered with the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA) pursuant to Title 21, 807 et seq. of the United States Code of Federal Regulations and is compliant with FDA 510(k) requirements.
