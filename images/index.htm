        AS-OBGYN

        AS-OBGYN™ delivers state-of-the-art ultrasound reporting and image management designed for women's healthcare and tailored for Maternal Fetal Medicine (MFM). AS-OBGYN is designed to be intuitive and easy to use, allowing you to finalize your reports with embedded images in seconds.Learn More

        AS-VASCULAR

        AS-VASCULAR™ generates comprehensive structured ultrasound reports and consultation letters in minutes. Eliminate dictation by configuring pre-defined templates and built-in macros to automate your impressions.Learn More

        AS-RADIOLOGY

        AS-RADIOLOGY™ delivers state-of-the-art structured ultrasound reporting designed for radiology. Utilize it as a stand-alone solution or fully integrate with your PACS and dictation systems to finalize your reports with embedded images in minutes.Learn More

        AS-IMAGE

        AS-IMAGE™ delivers a state-of-the-art ultrasound image management and archival solution that is scalable, robust, and efficiently manages ultrasound images. AS-IMAGE is vendor neutral and compatible with all ultrasound equipment manufacturers. AS-IMAGE will streamline your image management and ultrasound reporting workflows to maximize productivity and enhance patient care.Learn More

        AS-INTERFACES

        AS Software specializes in integrated solutions for maximum interoperability with healthcare information systems (e.g., HIS, RIS, PACS, EMR, practice management, billing and dictation). Unlike other software providers, our expertise allows us to build, deliver and maintain our own interfaces that are scalable, robust and compliant with DICOM & HL7 standards.Learn More

        AS-PERINATAL

        AS-PERINATAL™ EMR is intuitive, easy to use and emulates an MFM workflow, utilizes a structured clinical database and seamlessly integrates with your healthcare information systems to enable interoperability across platforms. This solution provides the tools to create a paperless environment, improve efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance perinatal care.Learn More
