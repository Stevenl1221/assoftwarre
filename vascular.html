AS-VASCULAR™ Ultrasound Reporting & Image Management for Vascular

AS-VASCULAR™ delivers state-of-the-art Vascular ultrasound reporting and image management. It is a scalable and robust solution utilizing a structured, clinical database to efficiently manage ultrasound information. AS-VASCULAR is designed to be intuitive and easy to use. The optional integrated image management solution allows you to finalize reports with embedded images in seconds.

AS-VASCULAR seamlessly integrates with your health information systems (e.g., HIS, RIS, PACS, EHR and EMR) to enable interoperability across platforms. AS-VASCULAR is vendor neutral and compatible with all ultrasound equipment manufacturers. AS-VASCULAR will provide you with the tools to perform intelligent data mining and statistical analysis to support your practice management, accreditation reviews, academic presentations and research. AS-VASCULAR is FDA 510(k) approved and compliant with HIPAA Privacy & Security requirements. AS-VASCULAR will streamline your ultrasound reporting and image management workflows to maximize productivity and enhance patient care to deal with today's healthcare challenges.

Simple and Unique Intuitive User Interface

AS-VASCULAR offers an intuitive user interface that is both logical and simple to use. You do not have to be computer literate to use and administer the system. Generate comprehensive, structured reports without endlessly toggling and jumping between multiple windows. Review reports in an easy-to-read, structured format that is linked directly to the ultrasound images for reviewing in high resolution DICOM format. Our User Interface will allow you to easily compare today’s images with previous studies. Embed your selected images and finalize your reports with just a few clicks of a mouse. AS-VASCULAR will streamline your operations, reduce users’ learning curves, simplify administration, and minimize system maintenance.

Customizable to Meet Your Needs

AS-VASCULAR is a configurable solution that emulates the workflow of reporting and image management in a Vascular Lab. Our technology will enable you to: configure default values and define your standard terminology throughout the system; define protocols for normal and abnormal findings; use your preferred references for calculations and normal ranges; automatically generate impressions, recommendations and consultation letters utilizing pre-defined templates and built-in macros that correctly format your current exam findings.

Efficient Management of Ultrasound Information & Images

AS-VASCULAR provides the tools to streamline the management of your ultrasound information and images. AS-VASCULAR will enable you to remotely review, modify, and electronically sign your finalized ultrasound reports from anywhere using a web browser. AS-VASCULAR will enable you to automatically fax your report, including embedded images to your referring physicians, while simultaneously transmitting a copy to your healthcare information systems (e.g., HIS, RIS, PACS, EHR and EMR) for storage.

Create Comprehensive Ultrasound Reports in Seconds

AS-VASCULAR enables you to create comprehensive, structured ultrasound reports and consultation letters in seconds. By communicating directly with your ultrasound modalities, AS-VASCULAR will automatically transfer measurements for inclusion in your finished report. In addition, AS-VASCULAR will automatically incorporate CPT and ICD codes when you document procedures, indications, and diagnoses. Use AS-VASCULAR to quickly generate professional reports with embedded ultrasound images. Electronically sign and automatically distribute your finalized ultrasound reports to your referring physicians and designated health information systems. AS-VASCULAR is your tool to create comprehensive ultrasound reports in seconds and provide proper documentation to protect your practice and increase your reimbursements.

Intelligent Data Mining & Accreditaion Support

AS-VASCULAR is built on a robust, relational SQL database and provides the tools necessary for intelligent data mining and statistical analysis in support of your practice management, accreditation reviews, and research & publication projects. Use the statistical summaries module for your daily administrative reporting needs. Use our "Friendly Query Builder" to easily extract your data without being an expert in computer programming and database structures. AS-VASCULAR comes packaged with a comprehensive library of hundreds of pre-defined, custom reports to help you analyze your data. Our export module allows you to export your data to other systems (e.g., Access®, Excel®, Crystal® reports, or PowerPoint®).

Use it as a stand-alone solution or fully integrate with your hospital or enterprise systems. We have extensive experience building complex, vendor-neutral interfaces. Seamless PACS Integration

AS-VASCULAR provides the tools for seamless PACS integration and efficient ultrasound image storage. Seamlessly integrate via API/XML plug-ins, DICOM query and DICOM send. The integrated AS-IMAGE™ module will automate your image archival needs. This completely configurable system allows you to efficiently manage short-term storage utilizing local storage, NAS, or SAN, and long-term storage utilizing your hospital or enterprise PACS.

Seamless Ultrasound Modality Integration

AS-VASCULAR is vendor neutral and compatible with all ultrasound equipment manufacturers. It is fully DICOM compliant and supports single-frame, cine loops and 3D/4D/5D volumes. Use DICOM modality work lists to upload scheduled patient data to your ultrasound machine. Automatically download ultrasound measurements via DICOM-SR or offline serial/parallel/USB communication. AS-VASCULAR provides a fully integrated solution that streamlines data entry and reduces errors.
